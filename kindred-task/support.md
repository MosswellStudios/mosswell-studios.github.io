## Frequently asked questions

### How do I manage or cancel my Kindred Pro subscription?

Go to **Settings → [your Apple ID] → Subscriptions** on your iPhone or iPad and select Kindred Task. You can cancel or change your plan there. Changes take effect at the end of the current billing period.

### How do I restore my subscription on a new device?

Open Kindred Task, go to **Settings → Subscription**, and tap **Restore Purchases**. Your subscription will be restored as long as you're signed in with the same Apple ID.

### Is my data backed up?

If iCloud sync is enabled (Settings → iCloud → Kindred Task), your tasks are automatically backed up and kept in sync across your Apple devices via Apple's encrypted CloudKit service.

### Does AI run on my device or in the cloud?

All AI features — including Voice Capture parsing and Kindred Suggest — run entirely on your device using Apple Intelligence (Apple's Foundation Models framework). Your tasks and notes are never sent to any server.

### How do I use Voice Capture?

Tap the microphone button in the Quick Capture sheet. Speak your task naturally — for example, "Remind me to finish the Q2 report by Friday, high priority." Kindred Task will parse your speech into a structured task with the correct priority, due date, and flag.

Voice Capture requires iOS 18.1 or later and a compatible device with Apple Intelligence enabled.

### How does Kindred Suggest work?

Kindred Suggest analyses your task list — priority, deadlines, focus history — and proposes a daily focus list using on-device AI. Open it from the **Plan My Day** button in the toolbar. All processing happens on your device; no data leaves it.

### How do I add a location-based reminder?

Open a task in the detail panel and tap **Add Reminder**. Select **Location**, then search for or pin a place on the map. You can set the reminder to trigger on arrival or departure within a chosen radius.

Location reminders require "Always" or "While Using" location permission granted in **Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services → Kindred Task**.

### The Focus Timer isn't saving my time — what should I do?

Make sure you stop the timer using the Stop button rather than force-quitting the app. Time is credited to the task when the timer is stopped or completes. Your total focus time appears in the Stats view.

### How do I import tasks from Todoist?

Go to **Settings → Data → Import via CSV / JSON**. Export your tasks from Todoist as a CSV file, then paste the contents into the import sheet. Kindred Task will map projects, tags, and priorities automatically.

### I'm not seeing my widgets — how do I add them?

Long-press your Home Screen, tap **+**, and search for "Kindred Task". Today and Flagged widgets are available in small and medium sizes, and lock screen sizes.
