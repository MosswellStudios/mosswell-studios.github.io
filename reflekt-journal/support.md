## Frequently Asked Questions

**How do I restore my subscription?**
Open Settings in the app and tap "Restore Purchases", or go to iPhone Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions → Reflekt Journal.

**My data isn't syncing across devices — what should I do?**
Go to Reflekt Journal → Settings → iCloud Sync and make sure it's turned on. Also check that iCloud Drive is enabled in iPhone Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud.

**How do I cancel my subscription?**
Go to iPhone Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions → Reflekt Journal → Cancel Subscription.

**Is my journal data private?**
Yes. All entries are stored on your device. AI features run entirely on-device using Apple's Foundation Models — nothing is sent to our servers or any external AI service. See our [Privacy Policy](./privacy-policy) for full details.

**Can I export my journal entries?**
Yes. Open any entry, tap the share icon, and choose Export as PDF to save or share your entry.

**How do I enable Face ID lock?**
Go to Settings within the app and toggle on App Lock. You will be prompted to authenticate with Face ID or your passcode.

**The AI features aren't working — what should I do?**
AI features require iOS 26 or later and use Apple's on-device Foundation Models. Make sure your device is updated to the latest version of iOS.

**I have a question not listed here.**
Email us at support@reflektjournal.com and we'll get back to you as soon as possible.
